# PalmTechnIQ Adjusted Pricing Framework (70/30 Installment Plan)

To provide flexibility while ensuring commitment, PalmTechnIQ offers a 2-installment payment option for all programs. Under this plan, **70% of the total installment cost is due at enrollment**, with the remaining **30% due as the second installment**.

## Crash Courses (1 Month)

**Base Price (Full Payment):** ₦90,000
**Installment Plan (2 Payments - Total ₦95,000):**

- **1st Installment (70% at enrollment):** ₦66,500
- **2nd Installment (30% after 2 weeks):** ₦28,500

_Applies to: Digital Marketing, Backend Development, Frontend Development, Fullstack Development, Data Analytics, Cybersecurity Smart, Home Automation Mobile, App Development Project, Management UI/UX, Design Product, Management._
